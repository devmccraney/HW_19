A Little About Me

I am a Tampa-based UX Researcher aiming to solve design problems, empathize with users, and create delightful experiences.

My background is in Mathematics, which may seem pretty distanced from UX. However, my education led to mastery in thinking outside the box and collaborating with others to solve complex problems. UX is my perfect blend of creativity and science.

I signed up for bootcamp to learn design, and fell in love with research instead. I love combining my strong problem solving skills with my natural empathy for others.

My UX Research Skills

Empathy

Persona building, creating user stories, journey mapping, interviewing, and testing.

Analysis

Performing usability heuristic evaluations, competitor analysis using SWOT method, and prototype feedback.

Data Synthesis

Combining qualitative data (user interviews, testing) with quantitative data (surveys, charts) to gain total insight into user experience.
